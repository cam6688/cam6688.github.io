WHO AM I?

Hello! My name is Cameron and welcome to my portfolio! As an aspiring creator, I am passionate about making all sorts of things - from art to coding projects (like this one!) Feel free to check things out, and contact me if you have any questions :)

Programmer

As an intended Computer Science major at UC Berkeley, I love to code! In the past, I've done front-end programming for most of my personal projects, but have ventured into backend in the past year and love it! I also love creating video games (especially choose your own adventure visual novels) and hope to make more later this year.

Art, Graphic Design

I also plan to double major in Art Practice at UCB, with a focus on film, graphics and animation. My favorite medium is digital drawing with Procreate or CLIP Studio Paint, followed by oil paint and charcoal. I have also picked up 3-D animation and graphic design in the past two years, and I am excited to continue building my skills in using these applications.

Animation, Makeup, Photography

Some of my smaller but important hobbies are in photography, videography and makeup artistry. These skills build off of my graphic design and art skills, yet can be considered as attributes in their own right. I hope to start a YouTube channel this year to develop and showcase these skills.

WHO AM I?

Hello! My name is Cameron and welcome to my portfolio site! As an aspiring creator, I am passionate about making all sorts of things - from art to coding projects (like this one!) Feel free to check things out, and contact me if you have any questions :)

Programmer

As an intended Computer Science major at UC Berkeley, I love to code! In the past, I've done front-end programming for most of my personal projects, and am now focusing on school projects. I've been learning a lot and hope to continue honing my skills! I also love creating video games (especially choose your own adventure visual novels) and am hoping to make more later this year.

Art, Graphic Design

I plan to double major in Art Practice at UCB, but so far most of my work is from high school, where I took AP Studio Art. My favorite medium is digital (using Procreate and/or CLIP Studio Paint), followed by acrylic and oil paint. Recently, I also started studying graphic design when I took the Intro to Photoshop and Illustrator decal with Innovative Design, a club on campus. I am excited to continue building my skills in using these applications.

Animation, Makeup, Photography

Some of my smaller but important hobbies are in photography, videography and makeup artistry. These skills build off of my graphic design and art skills, yet can be considered as attributes in their own right. I hope to start a YouTube channel this year to develop and showcase these skills.

"Mind(ful)less"

ArtAcrylic Painting

For this piece, I was inspired by pop art, political cartoons, and SNL's Weekend Update segments from the past year. Using clippings from my school newspaper and TIME magazines I had read, I made a composition that focused on the current political climate at the time (late 2017) and how people tended to downplay the world's issues. I used my piece, which I called "Mind(ful)less," to tell people to face what was happening in the world, and rally them to take action. My piece was displayed at an art show named "Face It," with an article about the show here.

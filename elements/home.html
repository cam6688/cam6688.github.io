WHO AM I?

Hello! My name is Cameron and welcome to my portfolio site! As an aspiring creator, I am passionate about making all sorts of things - from art to coding projects (like this one!) Feel free to check things out, and contact me if you have any questions :)

Programmer

As an intended Computer Science major at UC Berkeley, I love to code! In the past, I've done front-end programming for most of my personal projects. There's not much to show you just yet but there will be more soon! I also love creating video games (especially choose your own adventure visual novels) and am planning to release more later this year.

Art, Graphic Design

I plan to minor in Art Practice at UCB, but so far most of my work is from high school, where I took AP Studio Art. My favorite medium is digital, followed by acrylic and oil paint. Recently, I also started studying graphic design when I took the Intro to Photoshop and Illustrator decal with Innovative Design, a club on campus. I am excited to continue building my skills in using these applications.

Animation, Makeup, Photography

Some of my smaller but important hobbies are in photography, videography and makeup artistry. These skills build off of my graphic design and art skills, yet can be considered as attributes in their own right. I hope to start a YouTube channel this year to develop and showcase these skills.
